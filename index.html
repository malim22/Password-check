Learn how to protect yourself from cyber-attacks and improve your digital safety.

What is Cyber Security?

Cyber Security involves protecting computer systems and networks from cyber threats, such as hacking, data breaches, and malware. It is crucial to ensure personal and organizational safety in the digital world.

How to Protect Yourself:

  • Use strong, unique passwords for different accounts.
  • Be cautious of phishing emails and suspicious links.
  • Keep your software and systems updated.
  • Use a Virtual Private Network (VPN) for secure internet browsing.
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) where possible.
